# High redshift

Papers on the high-redshift universe — early galaxies, reionization, first light, $z \gtrsim 6$ observations and theory.

## P1 — read soon

### Reviews (foundation reading)
- [ ] **Fan, Bañados & Simcoe 2023** *(review)* — *Quasars and the Intergalactic Medium at Cosmic Dawn* — ARA&A 61, 373; written by ASPIRE co-authors, covers the exact quasar+IGM science landscape ASPIRE operates in. — [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2212.06907)
- [ ] **Robertson 2022** *(review)* — *Galaxy Formation and Reionization: Key Unknowns and Expected Breakthroughs by JWST* — ARA&A 60, 121; the pre-JWST scorecard that current results are filling in. — [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2110.13160)
- [ ] **Overzier 2016** *(review)* — *The Realm of the Galaxy Protoclusters* — A&ARv 24, 14; the Overzier review cited throughout Wang+23. — [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/1610.02099)

### ASPIRE follow-ups
- [ ] **Champagne+2024a** — *A Quasar-Anchored Protocluster at z=6.6 in ASPIRE: I. [O III] Emitters in a 10 Mpc Overdensity* — Cycle-2 follow-up to Wang+23 mapping the full extent of the J0305−3150 protocluster. — [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2410.03826)
- [ ] **Champagne+2024b** — *A Quasar-Anchored Protocluster at z=6.6 in ASPIRE: II. Environmental Analysis of Galaxy Properties* — Companion paper; SFR, mass, and environment trends inside the overdensity. — [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2410.03827)
- [ ] **Jin+2024** — *ASPIRE: JWST Supports Earlier Reionization around [O III] Emitters* — IGM Lyα forest transmission vs. [O III] galaxies across 14 ASPIRE fields. — [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2410.01318)

## P2 — this semester

### Reviews
- [ ] **Inayoshi, Visbal & Haiman 2020** *(review)* — *The Assembly of the First Massive Black Holes* — ARA&A 58, 27; modern reference on SMBH seed formation (heavy seeds, Pop III remnants, direct collapse). — [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/1911.05791)
- [ ] **McQuinn 2016** *(review)* — *The Evolution of the Intergalactic Medium* — ARA&A 54, 313; rigorous IGM physics reference, pairs with Fan+23. — [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/1512.00086)
- [ ] **Stark 2016** *(review)* — *Galaxies in the First Billion Years After the Big Bang* — ARA&A 54, 761; foundational pre-JWST review for z > 6 galaxies.

### ASPIRE / observational
- [ ] **Yang+2023** — *ASPIRE: A First Look at the Rest-frame Optical Spectra of z>6.5 Quasars Using JWST* — Hβ-based SMBH masses and Eddington ratios for 8 reionization-era quasars. — [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2304.09888)
- [ ] **Lin+2024** — *Broad-line AGN at z=4–5 Revealed by JWST/NIRCam WFSS* — 16 "little red dot" broad-line Hα AGN from ASPIRE fields — directly relevant to WFSS source classification.
- [ ] **Wu+2023** — *ASPIRE: JWST Discovers an Overdensity around a Metal Absorption-selected Galaxy at z~5.5* — Connection between metal absorbers and galaxy overdensities in ASPIRE fields.
